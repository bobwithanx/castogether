CAST #2: Restoration

Saturday, October 15, 2016

Where are we as people, communities, the Church, and creation broken? What is keeping us from restoration? How can we transcend these obstacles to embrace and advocate wholeness through our Savior?

These were some of the conversations and concepts we explored together at this three-hour event on October 15, 2016. A portion of ticket proceeds was donated to Neighborhood Ministries. Listen to the CAST #2: Restoration audio of the first-half presentations.
